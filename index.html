ABOUT ME

I am a London-based Front End Web Developer with almost 2 years experience in designing and developing websites from concept to roll out.

Since I moved to Uk in the 2011 I've worked in the Hospitality industry starting as Barista and becoming Assistant Manager in the last 5 years for different Cafes and Restaurants.

During this work experience I had to collaborate with many people in different roles and this gave me an organizational mindset.

My strong attention to detail has also been an asset that helped me to start my careeer as Web Developer. After realising my aptitude for web design and development I decided to professionalise what had started as a passion and hobby and embarked upon a career as a Web Developer.

I collaborated as Freelancer for an E Commerce / Digital Marketing Agency based in Italy with which I gained experience on Wordpress, Shopify and WooCommerce.
